Speak in Sentences

Verbs

Character Action
c change
d delete
v visually select
y yank (copy)
p put (paste)
. redo

Nouns

Character Meaning
$ end of line
^ beginning of line
w word
s sentence
b block

Modifiers

Character Meaning
t[letter] to [letter]
i inside
5 do 5 times
[verb twice] execute on the entire line

Putting It Together

Keystrokes Translation
d$ delete to the end of the line
ciw change inside word
ci" change inside quotes
. do that again
vt< visually select to carat
yy yank current line
5p put yanked text into buffer 5 times
3dd delete current line three times
